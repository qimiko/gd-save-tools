Geometry Dash save data tools

Your save data can typically be found in the following locations:
  • Windows: %localappdata%/GeometryDash
  • macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/GeometryDash
  • Android: /data/data/com.robtopx.geometryjump
Both CCLocalLevels.dat and CCGameManager.dat are supported.
